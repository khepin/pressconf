What is this?

PressConf is a website to let you hold video press conferences online. You setup a press conference, others can join it and listen to what you have to say.

Only you the creator of the conference is allowed to talk by default. Other participants can request you to let them ask a question in which case you will see a button next to their name. By clicking this button you give them the ability to broadcast their question to all the attendees of the conference. You can stop their question at any time by clicking the button that appears after they start talking.

Should I use it?

Most probably no! Not in its current stage. This is just a demo, it is buggy and not very secure in the current stage. Use it if you want to have fun and play around. Not more.
